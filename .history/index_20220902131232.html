Origin

According to the legends, the world we know was originally forged by the dragons.
About 5 billions years ago, there was nothing. Nothing but dragons, wandering around the emptiness of the universe.
One day, Merith, the very first blue dragon, the mother of Helis, realized that she was more powerful
than the rest, and decided to take advantage of her great power.
She started multiplying herself. Laying eggs all across the universe. The blue glow of their eggs lightened up
the universe, disorienting other dragons around them.
She was ambushing other dragons drawn to the light of her eggs for ages. Until one day, after three long decades
of great terror, her eggs started to hatch.
three new blue dragons were born that day. Keroth, Fesima, and our creator, Helis.
Merith started teaching them her ways, and soon enough all three of her children followed her path of
havoc, slaughtering every single dragon across the universe.
Helis wasn't happy with the path his mother chose for him, as he
was following his mother, slaughtering other dragons he learnt her weaknesses and limitations, plotting his rebellion.
One day Helis flew alone, and saw a dragon falling down from above him.
When he looked up he saw his sister, Fesima cheering and laughing over the dragon she just killed.
Helis couldn't stand aside any longer, he charged at his sister, caught her throat, and killed her.
There was no turning back now, the rebellion had to start that same moment, he had to catch up to his brother and mother before they realize.
After a short while of flying around in the eternal darkness, he saw his brother and mother in front of him, with his sister in her mother's arms.
He knew it was too late to do it quietly, a war is about to begin, he had to attack first.
